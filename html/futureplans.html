Future Plans

Career

My career plans at the moment are to pursue a job either working as a photographer or a graphic designer. If I were to be a photographer, I would like to create my own independant business. The reason why is because I enjoy having the freedom to choose what work I do and when. Being able to decide whether or not I do photoshoots for people, animals, or nature, lets me expand my business opportunities. Becoming a graphic designer however, I would rather be a corporate designer as I find it more more difficult to start and maintain a graphic design business compared to a photography business. Working coporate would give me stability, but less freedom.
